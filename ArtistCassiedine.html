Cassiedine Roy


Cassiedine Roy, born on October 28, 1986, in Las Piñas, Zapote, is a skilled artist at Grayscale Tattoo Collective. Specializing in traditional tattoos, Cassiedine has been creating vibrant and timeless pieces for the past 7 to 8 years. Driven by a hobby for drawing and a deep passion for art, Cassiedine has seamlessly translated his love for sketching into the world of tattooing. His favorite artwork, a colored peacock, exemplifies his ability to infuse traditional designs with rich colors and intricate details. With years of experience and a strong portfolio, Cassiedine continues to bring his artistic visions to life, offering clients stunning tattoos that reflect his dedication to the craft. At Grayscale Tattoo Collective, he is devoted to providing exceptional tattoo experiences that celebrate the beauty of traditional artistry.
