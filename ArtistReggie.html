Reggie Dato-on


Reggie Dato-on, born on August 5, 1992, in P. Burgos, is a talented artist at Grayscale Tattoo Collective. Specializing in realism tattoos, Reggie has been perfecting his craft for the past ten years, starting his tattoo journey in 2014. Fueled by a passion for art and a deep appreciation for drawing, Reggie has translated his love for sketching onto the canvas of human skin. His favorite styles include portraits and realism, where his skills truly shine, capturing lifelike details and emotions with incredible precision. With a decade of experience and an impressive portfolio, Reggie continues to bring his artistic vision to life, creating stunning tattoos that reflect both his talent and his dedication to the art form. At Grayscale Tattoo Collective, he is committed to providing clients with extraordinary tattoo experiences
