Billy Jay Villanueva


Billy Jay Villanueva, born on September 28, 1991, in P. Burgos, is the proud owner of Grayscale Tattoo Collective. Specializing in black and gray tattoos, Billy has honed his craft to perfection since beginning his tattoo journey in 2010. Driven by a deep passion and hobby for the art form, Billy has developed a keen eye for detail and a unique style that resonates with clients. Portraits are his favorite type of artwork, showcasing his ability to capture the essence and personality of each subject with stunning realism. With years of experience and a portfolio that speaks volumes, Billy continues to push the boundaries of tattoo artistry, transforming skin into beautiful canvases of black and gray masterpieces. At Grayscale Tattoo Collective, he leads a team dedicated to delivering exceptional tattoo experiences for every client.
